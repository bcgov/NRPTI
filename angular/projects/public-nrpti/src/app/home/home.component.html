Welcome to the Natural Resource Compliance and Enforcement Database

B.C.’s natural resource agencies are responsible for the protection, management and conservation of the province’s water, land, air and living resources. Use this site to view and learn more about the many activities undertaken by the Province every day to ensure compliance with regulatory requirements.

FIND RECORDS

What you can expect to find:

On this site you’ll find records, documents and details of compliance and enforcement activities undertaken by natural resource agencies such as administrative sanctions, administrative penalties, court convictions, inspections, orders, violation tickets and community environmental justice forums. Filter your results by name or company, dates, laws, and availability of documents.

B.C.’s natural resource agencies include:

  • Ministry of Agriculture
  • Ministry of Environment and Climate Change Strategy
  • Ministry of Energy, Mines, and Low Carbon Innovation
  • Ministry of Forests, Lands and Natural Resource Operations and Rural Development
  • Agricultural Land Commission
  • Environmental Assessment Office
  • Oil and Gas Commission

How to make the most of this site:

This site can be used to search for compliance and enforcement actions issued by the natural resource agencies. Where available, view associated documentation.

What does this all mean:

Publicly reporting and providing public access to compliance and enforcement records promotes compliance with laws and regulations. This site provides information to the public in meaningful ways through an intuitive interface.
